EU Product Safety & Compliance Information

Ali Tunç develops and operates all titles listed on this page, including Bilirim, Tabu (iOS and Android), Harf Alayım!, Easy Contacts, Triblox, and ali2nc.com. Users can reach Ali directly at hello@ali2nc.com for any legal, safety, or compliance questions.
