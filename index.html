About

I was introduced to Bitcoin and cryptocurrencies in 2017, and I have been building, teaching, and connecting people in Web3 ever since. Since 2020, I have worked full-time across the ecosystem—guiding go-to-market efforts, shaping product strategy, and translating complex ideas into momentum.

My roles lean into business development, yet my technical background keeps me close to product and engineering teams. From cross-border launches to hackathons and flagship events, I thrive where experimentation meets execution. Along the way I have built long-standing partnerships, unlocked new revenue streams, and helped brands show up authentically in crypto-native communities.

Whether it is a new venture or a scale-up navigating rapid change, I bring a global network, a strategic mindset, and the ability to ship fast.
